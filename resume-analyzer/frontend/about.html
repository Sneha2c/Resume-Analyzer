About Resume Analyzer

Our Mission

Resume Analyzer is an AI-powered platform designed to help job seekers optimize their resumes and increase their chances of landing their dream jobs. We use advanced keyword matching and scoring algorithms to provide actionable feedback.

How It Works

  • Upload your resume in PDF or DOC format
  • Our AI analyzes content, structure, and keywords
  • Receive detailed feedback and improvement suggestions
  • Get a comprehensive score breakdown

Technology Stack

Built with modern web technologies including HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript, PHP, and MySQL to deliver a seamless and powerful resume analysis experience.
